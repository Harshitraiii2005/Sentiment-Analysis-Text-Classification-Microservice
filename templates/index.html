Model Performance Benchmarks
Sentiment Model

Model: nlptown/bert-base-multilingual-uncased-sentiment (5-star)

  • English: 67% exact accuracy | 95% off-by-1 accuracy (product reviews)
  • Other languages (DE, FR, ES, NL, IT): 57–61% exact | 93–95% off-by-1
  • Hindi / Hinglish: Strong cross-lingual transfer (no native fine-tuning). Performs well on code-mixed text in practice.
Topic Classification Model

Model: MoritzLaurer/mDeBERTa-v3-base-mnli-xnli (Zero-shot)

  • XNLI Average Accuracy (15 languages): ~81%
  • English NLI: Very strong (~85–87% on MNLI-style tasks)
  • Multilingual Zero-shot: Excellent for 100+ languages including Hindi
  • Strength: High-quality natural language inference backbone → reliable topic ranking
Note: These are reported benchmark numbers from the original model cards. Real-world performance on Indian social media / Hinglish text is generally very good due to multilingual pre-training, but may vary depending on domain and code-mixing intensity.
